# Positioning & Disclaimers

## Honest Product Positioning

Budget Engineer occupies a specific niche: **professional-grade local-first computational design for the early-stage AEC lifecycle**. It is not trying to be "Revit in the browser" or "AutoCAD replacement." It is a complementary tool that fills the gap between back-of-envelope sketching and full-blown BIM authoring.

### Intended Use Cases

| Use Case | Fit | Notes |
|----------|-----|-------|
| Early-stage schematic design | Strong | Brief → design → BOQ in minutes |
| Quantity surveying / cost planning | Strong | Region-aware cost databases, 55K+ items |
| Construction presentation | Good | Presentation boards, annotation, export |
| Training & education | Strong | Academy, reference cases, self-assessment |
| NGO / institutional procurement | Moderate | BOQ-ready, audit trail via transactions |
| Supervised pilot programs | Good | Pilot feedback, review, trends dashboards |
| Final structural engineering | **Not intended** | Requires licensed professional |
| Certified compliance approval | **Not intended** | Compliance check is advisory only |
| Large-team real-time collaboration | **Not intended** | Local-first; multi-user sync is opt-in |
| Proprietary CAD/BIM replacement | **Not intended** | Not a Revit/AutoCAD/ArchiCAD substitute |

### Allowed Claims

- "Professional-grade local-first AEC lifecycle platform"
- "Transforms plain-language briefs into 2D CAD, 3D BIM, and BOQ"
- "Region-aware cost estimation with 55,000+ cost items"
- "11 drawing types including site plan, elevations, sections, MEP"
- "6 skill-path academy for design and construction fundamentals"
- "Human review and signoff in the governance workflow"
- "P1–P5 readiness validation tiers"
- "Presentation boards with annotation and SVG/PNG/PDF export"
- "Offline-first architecture — no paid AI APIs required"

### Not-Allowed Claims

- ❌ "Licensed architect replacement"
- ❌ "Final structural signoff"
- ❌ "Automatic certified approval"
- ❌ "Proprietary CAD/BIM equivalence"
- ❌ "Fake SaaS/cloud collaboration"
- ❌ "Compliance certification tool"

## Disclaimer Text

The following disclaimer must be displayed prominently wherever demo projects or platform capabilities are presented:

> **Important Disclaimer**
>
> Budget Engineer is a computational design and quantity surveying tool. It provides early-stage estimates, schematic designs, and advisory compliance checks. All designs, quantities, and cost estimates must be reviewed and approved by a registered professional (architect, engineer, or quantity surveyor) before any construction or procurement action. Budget Engineer does not provide licensed architectural, engineering, or legal services.
