The demo pack is a curated collection of example projects, scenarios, and walkthrough materials that demonstrate Budget Engineer's professional-grade, local-first AEC lifecycle capabilities. It is designed for:

- **Product evaluation** — see what the platform can do before creating your own project

## Positioning Statement

Budget Engineer is a **professional-grade, local-first computational design platform** for the AEC lifecycle. It transforms plain-language building briefs into 2D CAD drawings, 3D BIM models, engineering quantities, and tender-ready BOQs — entirely in your browser, with no paid AI APIs required.

**What it is:**
- An AI-augmented design tool for early-stage and schematic building design
- A quantity surveying assistant with region-aware cost databases
- A presentation and review platform with boards, annotation, and export
- A governance-aware workflow tool with human review/signoff in the loop
- A learning platform with 6 skill paths for design and construction fundamentals

**What it is NOT:**
- NOT a replacement for a licensed architect or structural engineer
- NOT capable of providing final structural signoff or certified approval
- NOT a substitute for proprietary CAD/BIM software (AutoCAD, Revit, ArchiCAD)
- NOT a SaaS/cloud collaboration platform (local-first; sync is opt-in)
- NOT a compliance certification tool (compliance checking is advisory)
